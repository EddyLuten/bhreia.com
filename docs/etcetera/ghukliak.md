## Phrases

* Grintani, do you know this guy?
* *Grintani, wen tu yem pengga?*

* What do you want?
* *Qre tu weng?*

* Who are you?
* *Qro tu?*

* Come inside
* *Latta io*

* Do you speak Ghukliak?
* *Zem tu ghukliak?*

* I have to poop
* *Vu ta knep slek*
